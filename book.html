Transportation to/from the Island

Fly to Taniti on a small jet or propeller plane, or arrive by cruise ship during its weekly stop at Yellow Leaf Bay. Our expanding airport ensures easy access for your tropical escape.

Transportation on the Island

Explore Taniti with ease using public buses, taxis, or rental cars. Walk or bike through scenic towns, or hop on a private bus to access remote rainforest trails and quiet beaches.

Lodging on the Island

From budget hostels and cozy B&Bs to family-run hotels and a luxurious four-star resort, Taniti offers comfortable accommodations for every travel style and budget.
