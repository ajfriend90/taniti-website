Unique Architecture

Stroll past homes and buildings designed in Taniti’s indigenous architectural style, blending nature and history.

Art Galleries

Explore rotating exhibits from Tanitian artists—featuring sculpture, textiles, wood carvings, and paintings.

Island History Museum

Learn about Taniti’s past, from native traditions to colonial contact and the island’s journey to independence.

Cultural Celebrations

Attend festivals and parades that celebrate Taniti’s music, dance, crafts, and island spirit throughout the year.
