Natural wonders to behold

Explore stunning white-sand beaches, hike through lush rainforests, and visit Taniti’s active volcano for unforgettable views.

Exciting city nightlife

Enjoy pubs, a microbrewery, dance clubs, a movie theater, and an arcade—all in vibrant Taniti City and Merriton Landing.

Family fun on the Island

From snorkeling and zip-lining to bowling and a future golf course, Taniti offers adventure and joy for all ages.

Experience a unique culture

Discover native architecture, explore art galleries and museums, and experience the warm traditions of Taniti's island culture.
