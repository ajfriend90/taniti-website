Local Transportation

Home > Book > Local Transportation

Public Buses

City buses run daily from 5 a.m. to 11 p.m., offering affordable and reliable access across Taniti City.

Taxis

Call or hail a licensed taxi in Taniti City for quick, direct rides. Ideal for travelers with tight schedules.

Rental Cars

Reserve a rental car from a local agency near the airport to explore Taniti on your own schedule.

Bikes & Helmets

Rent bikes for a scenic and eco-friendly way to explore the island. Helmets are required and included.
